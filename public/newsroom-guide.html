◈ TRADEX NEWSROOM

MULTI-AGENT INTELLIGENCE PLATFORM — USER GUIDE v1.0

01 — WHAT IS THIS?

The TRADEX NEWSROOM is a visual AI operations center that shows you what 7 specialized trading agents are currently analyzing about the market — in real time. Think of it as a war room where each agent is a specialist sitting at their desk, studying a different aspect of the market and reporting their conclusion.

It is not a chart — it is a consensus dashboard. The goal is to give you a single glance at the overall market picture across trend, price action, risk, news, and execution readiness.

02 — THE 7 AGENTS

Each agent sits at a desk in the newsroom. Their desk monitor glows in their status color.

TREND
Higher Timeframe Bias. Reads the big picture — is price in an uptrend or downtrend on the daily/weekly? This is the foundation. If TREND is BULLISH, the market overall favors long positions.
PR.ACTION
Price Action / SMC Analyst. Looks for Break of Structure (BOS), liquidity sweeps, order blocks, and fair value gaps. When it says ALERT, it has spotted a key structural move happening right now.
MASTER
Coordinator / Decision Maker. The boss. It listens to all 6 other agents, weighs their signals, and produces the final verdict. If MASTER says NO TRADE, there is no clear setup even if some agents are bullish. If it says ARMED, a setup is forming.
RISK GATE
Risk Validator. Checks if conditions are safe to trade — spread, volatility, session timing, news risk. If it shows VALID, risk conditions are acceptable. If BLOCKED, do not trade regardless of what other agents say.
CONTRARIAN
Devil's Advocate. Actively looks for reasons not to trade — overextension, divergence, fake breakouts. When it's MONITORING, it's watching but not blocking. If it goes ALERT, reconsider your bias.
NEWS
Macro & News Monitor. Tracks scheduled economic events (NFP, CPI, FOMC) and breaking news that could move the market. MONITORING = no major event imminent. ALERT = high-impact event approaching, be careful.
EXECUTION
Trade Execution Readiness. The last check before a trade. ARMED means all pre-conditions for entry are met and the system is ready to execute. STANDBY means waiting for a trigger. It only fires after all other agents give the green light.

03 — AGENT STATUS COLORS

Each agent's desk glows in their current status color. Here is what each color means:

GREEN — Bullish / Valid

Agent sees a positive, go-ahead signal. Market structure or conditions favor a trade in the bullish direction.

AMBER — Alert / Caution

Something notable is happening. A key level has been hit, a sweep occurred, or a news event is near. Pay attention.

CYAN — Armed / No-Trade

Armed: ready to execute. No-Trade: conditions do not support a trade right now — wait.

PURPLE — Monitoring

Agent is watching but has no strong signal yet. Neutral state — not blocking, not confirming.

RED — Bearish / Blocked

Either bearish bias detected, or Risk Gate is blocking all trades. If Risk Gate goes red, do NOT enter a trade.

GREY — Idle / Offline

Agent has not run yet or has no data. Run the pipeline to activate it.

The three large screens on the back wall of the newsroom each show a different data layer:

◆ Market Intelligence

A global market structure map. Shows broad regional market zones and key signal pings — green for bullish zones, amber for contested areas, purple for bearish zones.

◈ TRADEX — Agent Consensus

The centerpiece. Shows a live bar chart for each of the 7 agents. The height of each bar = the agent's current conviction level (0–100%). Taller bar = stronger signal. Colors match each agent's status. This is the heartbeat of the system.

◆ Agent Status Board

A real-time list of all 7 agents with their name and current status label (BULLISH, ALERT, ARMED, etc). Gives you the fastest possible read at a glance.

05 — THE CONSENSUS SCORE

At the bottom of the newsroom you will see a CONSENSUS reading. This is calculated by counting agent votes:

  • BULLISH votes — agents in state: Bullish, Valid, Armed
  • NEUTRAL votes — agents in state: Monitoring, No-Trade, Idle
  • BEARISH votes — agents in state: Bearish, Blocked

Example: 4B · 2N · 1R means 4 agents bullish, 2 neutral, 1 bearish → overall BULLISH consensus.

06 — HOW TO USE IT (STEP BY STEP)

1
Open the Brain / AI Analysis page in TRADEX Terminal. The Newsroom is displayed at the top of this section.
2
Click "RUN PIPELINE" (top right of the Newsroom). This triggers all 7 agents to analyze the current market for your selected symbol and timeframe. Wait 3–5 seconds for results.
3
Read the CONSENSUS at the bottom strip first. Is it BULLISH, BEARISH, or NEUTRAL? This gives you the macro picture in one word.
4
Check the RISK GATE agent. If it is RED (BLOCKED), stop here — do not trade, regardless of what other agents say. Risk conditions are not met.
5
Check the MASTER agent. If it says NO-TRADE or is CYAN, there is no confirmed setup yet. If it says ARMED or BULLISH/BEARISH, a setup is active.
6
Check TREND and PR.ACTION. These confirm the directional bias. TREND = big picture, PR.ACTION = immediate price structure. Both should agree for a high-conviction trade.
7
Check NEWS and CONTRARIAN. If NEWS is ALERT, there is a high-impact event nearby — be cautious. If CONTRARIAN is ALERT, the market may be overextended — reduce size or wait.
8
EXECUTION = ARMED? If yes, and all the above checks pass, you have a high-probability setup. Proceed to your chart for exact entry.

07 — QUICK REFERENCE: WHAT TO DO

SCENARIO ACTION
RISK GATE = RED (Blocked) DO NOT TRADE. Close the chart.
CONSENSUS = NEUTRAL, MASTER = NO-TRADE WAIT. Market has no clear direction. Stand aside.
CONSENSUS = BULLISH, MASTER = ARMED, EXECUTION = ARMED HIGH CONVICTION LONG SETUP. Go to chart, look for entry.
TREND = BULLISH but PR.ACTION = ALERT CAUTION. Trend is up but price is at a key level — wait for confirmation.
NEWS = ALERT HIGH-IMPACT EVENT NEAR. Reduce position size or wait for the event to pass.
CONTRARIAN = ALERT while others are BULLISH POSSIBLE OVEREXTENSION. Be skeptical of new longs. Tighten stops.
All 7 agents = same color FULL CONSENSUS. Highest probability setup. Rare — take it seriously.

08 — HOW THE PIPELINE FLOWS

When you press RUN PIPELINE, the agents run in this order:

TREND → PR.ACTION → NEWS → RISK GATE → CONTRARIAN → MASTER → EXECUTION

Each agent passes its findings to the next. MASTER reads all 6 inputs before deciding. EXECUTION only activates if MASTER approves. This flow prevents false signals from individual agents triggering premature trades.

09 — GLOSSARY

BOS — Break of Structure

Price breaks a recent swing high (bullish BOS) or swing low (bearish BOS). Signals a shift in market direction.

SMC — Smart Money Concepts

A framework analyzing how institutional traders (banks, funds) move price — order blocks, fair value gaps, liquidity sweeps.

Liquidity Sweep

Price briefly breaks a key level to grab stop-loss orders from retail traders, then reverses. Often precedes a strong move.

HTF Bias

Higher Timeframe Bias — the dominant directional trend on daily/weekly charts. It filters out lower-timeframe noise.

Order Block

A price area where large institutional orders were placed. Price often returns to these zones, making them key support/resistance.

Pipeline

The sequence of all 7 agents running in order, each feeding results to the next, ending with a final MASTER decision.
