AGENT 4 — RISK GATE — SYSTEM OVERRIDE

The Risk Gate runs after the Execution Agent (Phase 2b) to receive the actual computed RR ratio. If any hard-block condition fires, the gate sets valid = false and the Master Agent issues NO TRADE — regardless of what Trend, Price Action, or News returned. There is no AI fallback, no confidence weighting, and no override path.

HARD-BLOCK CONDITIONS — ANY ONE FIRES = BLOCKED
  • Session closed (after-hours / Closed state)
  • Volatility > 2.5% daily move (ATR proxy exceeds extreme threshold)
  • RR ratio < 1:1 (actual RR from Execution Agent, not estimated)
  • 5 or more concurrent risk warnings accumulated
  • RSI deeply overbought (>78) or deeply oversold (<22)
